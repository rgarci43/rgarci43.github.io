M6- Site Planning Analysis Page

Website Goal

The goal of this website is to inform my target audience about automotive detailing and how its different from a regular carwash.

Working Title

I am thinking about having three pages. A home page, a survey page, and a gallery. The home page will have an introduction on what the page is about and explain what a detail is and the different products and techniques used and it will explain the whole process step by step. at the end of the page it will have pictures and then to see more you simply click on them to go to the gallery page. Also somewhere in there im gonna put a link to the form page so people can take a survey to see if the website helped them learn anything.

Forms

For forms i am going to add a survey so the users can submit information to say if they learned anything new or if the website helped them learn anything in any type of way.

Sources

The sources im going to use are from very reputable companies such as Chemical Guys, Meguires, Ammo Nyc their services have been here for years and i will get my images from them as well!
