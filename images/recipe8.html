M8 Recipe

Bacon ham and cheese omelet!

If you want more information email me at rgarci43@email.cpcc.edu

Ingredients Quantity
Large Grade A Eggs 3
Thick cut smoked bacon 3-4 slices
Sliced Ham 2 slices
Shredded Cheddar One handful
Salt A pinch of salt
pepper A pinch of pepper
Wheat Bread 2-3 slices
Preparation
First things first we gotta cook that bacon and make it real crispy just throw the bacon into a pan and cook. take the bacon out onto a cutting board but leave that bacon grease in the pan thats what we're gonna use to cook the eggs with. Now with the bacon on the board dice it until its almost like a powder. While you're at it dice the ham as well. Now crack three eggs in a bowl and whisk it up. Then in that hot skillet pour the eggs into the pan then season. Make sure to bring down the sides when the solidify. Now add the bacon and the ham. By this point it should be done, sprinkle your cheese then fold 1/4 of the egg in and flip the rest over it. You should be greeted to a done omelet i like mines a little bit more well done but up to you. You could also add some toast because what psycho eats an omelet by itself? But after all that, enjoy!
